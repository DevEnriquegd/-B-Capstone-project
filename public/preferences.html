My Musical Preferences

Paramore

Paramore is an American rock band formed in Franklin, Tennessee, in 20041. The band’s members include lead vocalist Hayley Williams, lead guitarist Taylor York, and drummer Zac Farro. Williams and Farro are founding members of the group, while York, a high school friend of the original lineup, joined in 2007

The Marías

The Marías es una banda de indie pop originaria de Los Ángeles, California, formada en 20161. La banda está compuesta por la cantante principal María Zardoya, nacida en Puerto Rico y criada en Atlanta, Georgia, y el baterista Josh Conway, nativo de Los Ángeles. Son conocidos por interpretar canciones tanto en inglés como en español, y por infundir su música con elementos como percusión de jazz, riffs de guitarra, voces aterciopeladas y solos de trompeta

Twenty One Pilots

Twenty One Pilots is an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio, formed in 20091. The group is composed of Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun. They have achieved great success performing at major festivals such as Lollapalooza, Bonnaroo, Boston Calling and Firefly.The duo is known for their unique blend of genres that includes elements of rock, hip-hop, punk and reggae2. Their introspective lyrics and energetic live performances have captivated audiences.
